Double Up

During a double up day votes are multiplied by two. These double ups run all day from midnight to midnight. Any votes on the double up day counts twice!

Tripple Up

Tripple Ups run for 3 hours from 11H30 to 14H30. All votes received in a tripple up round are multiplied by three. Really get ahead with three times as many votes.

Quad Up

During Quad Up rounds all votes are multiplied by four. These are the holy grail of power up rounds. Quad Ups run for four hours from 11H00 to 15H00.
