Bonus Top Up

During some competitions there may be a bonus top up prize. The bonus top up, when it occurs, is a progressive prize added to the first place winnings. If a bonus top up is activated for a competition then each new vote in that competition adds an amount to the bonus top up.
If, for instance, the bonus top up amount grows to R1243 and the first place prize is R5000, then the total first place winning will be R5000 + R1243 = R6243.

Voters Prize

Voters are entered into a draw to win a R2000 cash prize. Each vote is an entry into the draw so the more you vote the more your chances to win (eg, a 20 vote bundle gets you 20 entries into the draw).
NOTE: SMS voters are automatically entered. If you vote by credit card, paypal or snap scan you will need to register your vote.
